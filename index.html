Our Products

We offer a wide range of high-quality optical products to suit all ages and needs:

Ophthalmic Glasses

Our extensive collection of prescription eyewear includes frames for all ages, styles, and budgets. We carry leading brands and the latest designs to ensure you look and see your best.

Sunglasses

Protect your eyes with our range of prescription and non-prescription sunglasses. We offer options for adults and children, with a focus on UV protection and durability for active lifestyles.

Contact Lenses

We provide various types of contact lenses including daily disposables, monthlies, and specialty lenses for astigmatism and multifocal needs. Our optometrists will help you find the perfect fit.

Children's Eyewear

We specialize in optical requirements for children of all ages. Our kid-friendly frames are durable, comfortable, and designed to withstand active play while providing optimal vision correction.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of eye care services to meet all your vision needs:

  • Eye Examinations (may be bulk billed)
  • Spectacles (may be bulk billed to private Health Fund optical cover)
  • Diabetic eye examinations
  • Colour Vision assessments
  • Macular Degeneration tests
  • Glaucoma & eye pressure testing
  • Checks for Cataracts
  • Clearness of vision (myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism)
  • Focusing ability (Presbyopia)
  • Eye muscle movement and control
  • Amblyopia (lazy eye)
  • Contact lens fitting and management
  • Referrals for eye laser surgery
  • Mining & Industrial Safety Spectacles
  • Advice on fitting quality sunglasses (prescription and non-prescription)

About ABC Optical

We are a Brisbane based company which is well regarded locally. We employ two full-time, in-store qualified optometrists able to diagnose and treat a number of eye related problems.
